🎓 Still Looking for a Meaningful Internship? 🌟

6-Week Creative Internships

Three highly selective programs for creative, driven students who want to make real impact!

Create, Lead, and Grow!

We know many students are still searching for internships that are actually impactful — ones that let you create, lead, and grow. That's why FilmPsych is launching three highly selective, 6-week internship programs this early October!

🎨✨

Visual Storytelling Internship

For artists, illustrators, and aspiring animators

  • Independently create a short animated video in collaboration with a writer on a mental health/psychology script
  • Collaborate with a group of writers to illustrate a children's book on a psychological/emotional theme
  • Learn about storyboarding, visual emotion, and how art can effectively communicate mental health topics
  • Final books will be published on our website, and select titles will be published and sold on Amazon Kindle, with proceeds going toward our 501(c)(3) nonprofit and its impacts
Perfect for creative students who want to use art for advocacy, grow their storytelling skills, and gain an academic edge in applications and resumes!
✍️📖

Editorial & Creative Writing Internship

For writers, bloggers, and storytellers

  • Write a solo blog article and short script on a real-world mental health or emotional issue
  • Team up with artists to co-author a children's book on a psychological topic
  • Explore clarity, tone, and impactful narrative techniques
  • Final books will be published on our website, and top works will be published on Amazon Kindle, with proceeds going toward our 501(c)(3) nonprofit and its impacts
Perfect for passionate writers who want to explore psychology, connect with real readers, and gain an academic edge in applications and resumes!
🧠🔬

Psychology Research & Literature Review Internship

For curious, driven future researchers

  • Learn real research methods and write your own literature review on a psychological topic of your choice
  • Refine your final draft into a research paper with help from experienced student mentors and professionals in the field
  • Final papers will be published on our website, and we can help support official research journal submissions if applicable
  • Gain hands-on experience with academic research methodologies and scientific writing
Ideal for students interested in psychology, neuroscience, or gaining an academic edge for college applications!

Why These Programs Rock!

100% FREE! No cost to participate 🎉
Fully Virtual Work from anywhere in the world 🌍
Global Community Open to high school students worldwide 🚀
Small & Personal Only 10-20 students per program 💫

Amazing Mentorship & Support!

Each group will be guided by two experienced student mentors, with occasional visits from a professional in psychology or neuroscience to support your learning. Many students have already expressed interest, but we are only selecting 10-20 participants per program to ensure personalized attention and meaningful collaboration! 🌟
