6-Week Internship Programs

Hands-on experience in psychology and creative arts

About Our Internship Programs

FilmPsych offers comprehensive 6-week internship programs designed to provide hands-on experience in the intersection of psychology and creative arts. Our internships combine practical learning with creative expression, allowing participants to develop professional skills while contributing to meaningful mental health awareness initiatives.

Available Tracks

Editorial Track

Focus on content creation, blog writing, and editorial processes in psychology communication.

  • Blog writing and editing
  • Content strategy development
  • Research and fact-checking
  • Psychology communication skills

Art Track

Combine artistic skills with psychological concepts to create meaningful visual content.

  • Digital art and illustration
  • Animation and motion graphics
  • Visual storytelling techniques
  • Art therapy concepts

Final Projects

At the end of your internship, you'll complete one of several final projects to showcase your skills:

Blog Creation

Research, write, and publish a comprehensive blog article analyzing psychology in creative media.

Artwork/Animation

Create original artwork or animation that explores psychological concepts and mental health themes.

Children's Book

Design and create an educational children's book that teaches psychology concepts in an age-appropriate way.

Research Project

Conduct research and create a literature review on a specific topic in psychology and creative arts.
