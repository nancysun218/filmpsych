Meet Our Board Members

The dedicated leaders guiding FilmPsych's mission to bridge psychology and creative arts

Nancy Sun

President

Nancy Sun is the visionary founder and president of FilmPsych, bringing together her deep passion for psychology and creative storytelling. With a strong academic background in psychology and extensive experience in organizational leadership, Nancy identified the unique opportunity to bridge mental health awareness with popular media analysis. Her innovative approach has transformed how audiences engage with psychological concepts through film and television. Nancy's dedication to making psychology accessible and relatable has been the driving force behind FilmPsych's growth from a small initiative to a recognized platform for psychological education. She oversees strategic planning, content development, and maintains the organization's commitment to accurate, meaningful psychological analysis while fostering a supportive community for both creators and audiences.

Founder & President Psychology Expert Visionary Leader Innovation

Kiersten Oh

Volunteer Coordinator

Kiersten Oh brings exceptional organizational skills and a genuine passion for community building to her role as Volunteer Coordinator. With a background in human resources and team management, Kiersten has been instrumental in developing FilmPsych's volunteer program from the ground up. She specializes in matching volunteers with opportunities that align with their skills and interests, creating a fulfilling experience for everyone involved. Kiersten's warm and approachable leadership style has fostered a welcoming environment where volunteers feel valued and motivated to contribute their best work. She coordinates training sessions, manages volunteer schedules, and serves as the primary liaison between different departments. Her dedication to volunteer development has resulted in high retention rates and a strong sense of community within the organization.

Team Coordination Program Development Community Building Excellence in Leadership

Iris Liu

Volunteer Coordinator

Iris Liu is a dedicated Volunteer Coordinator who brings a unique blend of empathy, organizational expertise, and creative problem-solving to FilmPsych. With experience in educational psychology and volunteer management, Iris focuses on creating meaningful growth opportunities for volunteers while ensuring they feel supported throughout their journey with the organization. She has developed comprehensive onboarding processes and mentorship programs that help new volunteers integrate seamlessly into the FilmPsych community. Iris is particularly skilled at identifying individual strengths and providing personalized guidance to help volunteers maximize their impact. Her commitment to fostering an inclusive, collaborative environment has made her an invaluable asset to the team. She works closely with content creators to ensure volunteer contributions are recognized and celebrated, building a culture of appreciation and mutual support.

Volunteer Development Mentorship Program Excellence Collaboration

Aland Kim

Marketing Director

Aland Kim serves as FilmPsych's Marketing Director, bringing innovative digital marketing strategies and a deep understanding of social media trends to expand the organization's reach and impact. With a background in communications and digital marketing, Aland has been pivotal in establishing FilmPsych's distinctive brand voice across multiple platforms. He specializes in creating engaging content that resonates with diverse audiences while maintaining the educational integrity that defines FilmPsych's mission. Aland's strategic approach to marketing has significantly increased the organization's visibility, leading to substantial growth in followers and engagement across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. He collaborates closely with content creators to develop marketing campaigns that authentically represent FilmPsych's values while attracting new audiences to psychological education through creative media analysis.
