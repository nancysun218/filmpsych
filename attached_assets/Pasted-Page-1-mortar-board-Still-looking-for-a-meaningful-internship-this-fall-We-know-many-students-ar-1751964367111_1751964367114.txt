:mortar_board: Still looking for a meaningful internship this fall?
We know many students are still searching for internships that are actually impactful — ones that let you create, lead, and grow. That’s why FilmPsych is launching three highly selective, 6-week internship programs this early October.
Page 2:
:art: Visual Storytelling Internship
(For artists, illustrators, and aspiring animators)
:heavy_check_mark: Independently create a short animated video in collaboration with a writer on a mental health/psychology script
:heavy_check_mark: Collaborate with a group of writers to illustrate a children’s book on a psychological/emotional theme
:heavy_check_mark: Learn about storyboarding, visual emotion, and how art can effectively communicate mental health topics
:heavy_check_mark: Final books will be published on our website, and select titles will be published and sold on Amazon Kindle, with proceeds going toward our 501(c)(3) nonprofit and its impacts.
:dart: Perfect for creative students who want to use art for advocacy, grow their storytelling skills, and gain an academic edge in applications and resumes.

:writing_hand: Editorial & Creative Writing Internship
(For writers, bloggers, and storytellers)
:heavy_check_mark: Write a solo blog article and short script on a real-world mental health or emotional issue
:heavy_check_mark: Then, team up with artists to co-author a children’s book on a psychological topic
:heavy_check_mark: Explore clarity, tone, and impactful narrative techniques
:heavy_check_mark: Final books will be published on our website, and top works will be published on Amazon Kindle, with proceeds going toward our 501(c)(3) nonprofit and its impacts.
:dart: Perfect for passionate writers who want to explore psychology, connect with real readers, and gain an academic edge in applications and resumes.

:brain: Psychology Research & Literature Review Internship
(For curious, driven future researchers)
:heavy_check_mark: Learn real research methods and write your own literature review on a psychological topic of your choice.
:heavy_check_mark: Refine your final draft into a research paper with help from experienced student mentors and professionals in the field.
:heavy_check_mark: Final papers will be published on our website, and we can help support official research journal submissions if applicable.
:dart: Ideal for students interested in psychology, neuroscience, or gaining an academic edge for college.

These internships are 100% free, fully virtual, and open to high school students worldwide. Many students have already expressed interest, but we are only selecting 10-20 participants per program.
Each group will be guided by two experienced student mentors, with occasional visits from a professional in psychology or neuroscience to support your learning.
